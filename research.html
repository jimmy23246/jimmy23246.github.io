Research areas

Abstract

PSCL's research expands on two primary domains: powertrain systems (thermal science) and powertrain controls (automatic control). In partiular the powertrain systems research focuses on refining after-treatment model design based on artificial intelligence and implementation of deep learning data. This effort aims at increasing efficiency of traditional exhaust system and reducing greenhouse emission- which consolidate to actualizing our goal of materializing the greener future. 

The powertrain controls research aims at development of advanced catalyst control compositions and algorithms based on condition prediction and deep learning algorithms.
